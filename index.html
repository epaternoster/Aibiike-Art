How It Works

Send a Request

Fill out the commission form or DM me with your character details and commission type.

Approval & Quote

I'll review your request and send a final price quote within 1–3 days.

Payment

A 50% upfront payment is required before work begins. The remainder is due on completion.

Sketch Preview

I'll share a WIP sketch for your feedback before moving to final lineart and colour.

Final Delivery

Once the remaining payment is received, you'll get the high-resolution file.

Terms of Service

Payment Payment is via PayPal (invoiced). 50% upfront, 50% on completion. No refunds after lineart is approved.
Usage Rights You may use the artwork for personal, non-commercial purposes. Credit is appreciated. Commercial use requires a separate license — please ask.
Revisions Up to two minor revisions are included. Major changes or additional revisions may incur extra fees.
Turnaround Estimated 2–6 weeks depending on complexity and queue length. Rush slots may be available for an extra fee.
I Will Draw Original characters, fan art, anthro/furry, mild romance, and most fandom content.
I Will Not Draw NSFW, real people without consent, hate symbols, or anything I'm uncomfortable with. I reserve the right to decline any request.
